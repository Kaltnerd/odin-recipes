Vanilla mug cake

Treat yourself to this easy vanilla mug cake for one. Made in the microwave, it takes only a few minutes to make. Decorate with icing sugar and sprinkles

Ingredients

Yield:1 serving

  • 30g butter
  • 2 ½ tbsp caster sugar
  • 6 tbsp plain flour
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • 3 ½ tbsp milk
  • ½ tsp vanilla 4 tsp icing sugar sprinkles, to serve (optional)

PREPARATION

  1. Put the butter in a microwave-safe mug and microwave on high for 30-45 seconds until melted. Stir in the caster sugar, flour, baking powder, milk and vanilla until smooth.

  2. Microwave on high for 1 min-1 min 15 seconds until cooked through and a skewer inserted into the middle comes out clean. Set aside while you make the icing.

  3. Combine the icing sugar with enough water to create a drizzly consistency, then spoon over the cake (it will melt a little with the heat of the sponge). Scatter over the sprinkles, if using, and serve.
